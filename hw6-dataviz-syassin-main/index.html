Top Video Game Publisher by Region

1. The data obtained at the start provides us with many games made across the years from numerous publishers, as well as the sales of the game in each region of the world. My plots allow an individual to visualize what is arguably the most important facets of this data; that is, the best performing video games (as determined by volume of sales) and video game makers filtered by relevant categories such as region and genre. It allows you to see who is the top based on your location/genre preferences. The first graph is a barplot comparing the top 10 video games of all times. That is, it is a plot which compares the aggregate sales of the top 10 performing games ever, allowing you to see how they compare to eachother. The color gradient allows the viewer to differentiate a bit easier on the different video games, as well as emphasize that their is a difference in their popularity even though these are the top 10 most popular. The axis are labelled clearly to help the viewer comprehend what is being referred to. The second graph is a world map which has a label of the top genre (as measured by Sales) in each region of the dataset, which is labelled very clearly to ensure there is no confusion. I made sure to choose a light color for earth to help contrast the label so it can be seen clearly. The 3rd graph is a world map labelling the top publisher (also measured by sales) in each reason provided in the dataset. 2. My favorite part of D3 is how accessible it makes world maps.
